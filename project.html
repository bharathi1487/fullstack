INTERSTELLAR

Interstellar is a sci-fi movie directed by Christopher Nolan about a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. It explores themes of love, time, and survival across space and time.

INCEPTION

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, where a skilled thief enters peoples dreams to steal or plant ideas. The movie explores complex layers of dreams, reality, and the power of the subconscious mind.

OPPENHEIMER

Oppenheimer is a biographical drama directed by Christopher Nolan that tells the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the scientist who led the development of the atomic bomb during World War II. The film explores his moral dilemmas, scientific achievements, and the profound impact of nuclear weapons on humanity.

SUMMARY

  • Sci-fi movies explore futuristic technology, space travel, and advanced science concepts
  • They often imagine alternate realities, time travel, or alien life forms
  • Sci-fi films combine action, mystery, and philosophical questions about humanity future
